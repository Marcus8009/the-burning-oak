Getting Here

By Car

Parking is available in our private lot behind the restaurant. Additional street parking is available on Ember Lane and surrounding streets.

Public Transit

We are a 5-minute walk from the Central Station. Take the Blue Line to Central Station, exit east, and walk two blocks north.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept reservations?

Yes, we recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner service Thursday through Saturday. You can make a reservation by phone at (555) 123-4567.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Our menu indicates vegetarian and gluten-free options, and our chefs are happy to accommodate other dietary needs. Please let us know about any allergies or restrictions when you make your reservation or speak with your server.

Can you host private events?

Yes, we offer private dining options for groups and special events. We have a separate dining room that can accommodate up to 30 guests. Please contact us at least two weeks in advance to discuss your event needs.

Is there a dress code?

We suggest smart casual attire. While we don't enforce a strict dress code, most of our guests prefer to dress comfortably but elegantly for the dining experience.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, gift cards are available in any denomination. They can be purchased in person at the restaurant or by phone.
