Our Story

The passion behind The Burning Oak

Our Beginning

The Burning Oak was born from a simple yet profound vision: to create a dining experience that honors the precision and tradition of Japanese cuisine while embracing the boldness and comfort of Western flavors.

Founded in 2018 by Chef Kenji Williams and restaurateur Emma Chen, our restaurant stands at the intersection of two culinary worlds, creating something entirely new while respecting the traditions that came before.

Our Philosophy

The Transformative Power of Fire

At the heart of our culinary approach is the oak wood fire that gives our restaurant its name. Fire transforms ingredients, bringing out complex flavors and creating the perfect balance between raw and refined.

Harmony of Opposites

We believe in finding the perfect equilibrium between seemingly opposing elements: East and West, tradition and innovation, simplicity and complexity. This balance creates dishes that are more than the sum of their parts.

Respect for Ingredients

We source the finest seasonal ingredients from local farmers and sustainable suppliers. Each component of our dishes is treated with respect, allowing its natural qualities to shine while contributing to a greater whole.

Meet Our Team

Kenji Williams

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Born in Tokyo and raised in Seattle, Chef Kenji's multicultural background naturally led him to explore fusion cuisine. After training in traditional kaiseki restaurants in Kyoto and working in award-winning establishments across America, he brings a unique perspective that bridges two culinary worlds.

Emma Chen

Co-Founder & Director of Operations

With over 15 years in the hospitality industry and a background in business, Emma brings operational excellence and a warm, attentive approach to the dining experience. Her vision for The Burning Oak was to create not just a restaurant, but a journey for the senses.

Marco Santos

Head Chef

Trained in classical French techniques and passionate about American barbecue, Chef Marco found his perfect home at The Burning Oak. He brings creative energy and technical precision to our kitchen, specializing in our wood-fired creations.

Aiko Tanaka

Pastry Chef

A master of both Japanese and French pastry traditions, Aiko creates desserts that perfectly conclude our dining experience. Her innovative approach combines unexpected flavors and textures while maintaining the perfect balance that defines The Burning Oak.

Our Journey

2018

The Beginning

Kenji and Emma open The Burning Oak in a modest space, introducing their vision of Japanese-Western fusion to the city.

2019

First Recognition

The Burning Oak receives its first critical acclaim, named "Best New Restaurant" by Metro Magazine.

2020

Adapting & Evolving

During challenging times, we pivoted to create innovative to-go experiences, bringing The Burning Oak experience into homes.

2022

Expansion

We moved to our current, larger space, designed specifically to showcase the oak fire cooking that defines our cuisine.

2024

Growing Recognition

Chef Kenji is nominated for a prestigious James Beard Award, and The Burning Oak earns its first Michelin star.

Today

Continuing Our Journey

We continue to evolve and refine our vision, while staying true to the philosophy that has guided us from the beginning.

Our Values

  • Sustainability

    We are committed to sustainable practices, from sourcing ingredients to minimizing waste. Our oak wood comes from responsibly managed forests, and we work with suppliers who share our environmental values.

  • Community

    The Burning Oak is proud to be part of our local community. We participate in food festivals, support local causes, and create a welcoming space for all who wish to experience our unique cuisine.

  • Innovation

    While we honor tradition, we constantly explore new techniques, flavors, and presentations. Our menu evolves with the seasons and our ongoing culinary discoveries.

  • Excellence

    From the quality of our ingredients to the precision of our service, we strive for excellence in every aspect of The Burning Oak experience.
